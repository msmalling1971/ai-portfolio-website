# Project Vision

Version 1 should position Matt Smalling as a senior infrastructure modernization and operations leader who is deliberately moving into platform engineering and AI infrastructure.

The site should feel serious, technical, executive-level, and grounded in real systems work. The visual direction uses a graphite base, warm off-white text, restrained amber and steel-blue accents, architecture-diagram cues, timeline structures, and project cards written like engineering case studies.

The tone should communicate:

- Operational maturity
- Infrastructure modernization experience
- Platform engineering direction
- Hands-on AI infrastructure learning
- Executive communication and technical credibility

It should avoid generic developer portfolio patterns, neon AI aesthetics, startup buzzword overload, and fake futuristic visuals.

The story arc should read as:

1. Operational credibility
2. Modernization leadership
3. Whole-stack infrastructure judgment across data center, virtualization, cloud, security, backups, recovery, and modernization
4. Platform engineering transition as an evolution of that experience
5. Current initiatives
6. AI infrastructure lab as proof of active hands-on learning
7. Project architectures and case-study thinking
